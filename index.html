A Brief Introduction

Arm's Length is a pop-punk/midwest emo band from Ontario, Canada. The band was formed in 2018 with Jeremy Whyte on guitar, Jeff Whyte on drums, and Allen Steinburg on vocals and guitar. In 2022 Benjamin Greenblatt would join the band playing base.

In spring 2022, after Benjamin Greenblatt had joined the band, Arm's Length would release their first full length album 'Never Before Seen, Never Again Found'. Following the release of this album, the band would spend the next two years openning for larger bands in their genre.

These tours helped the band pick up traction, and Arm's Length would go on to play their first headlining tour in spring of 2024, along with opening bands Saturdays At Your Place, Carly Cosgrove, and Ben Quad.
